THE CLIENT

No loaders. No configs. Just play.

The launcher installs the right stack for whatever version you pick — Fabric on modern versions, Forge + OptiFine on the legacy ones — and pre-configures it. You never see a loader menu.

  • Performance, pre-tuned

    Sodium, Lithium and FerriteCore ship bundled and configured. Smoother frames and faster chunk loading, out of the box.

  • Shaders that just work

    Iris + Sodium on modern versions, OptiFine on the legacy pair. Drop in a shader pack from the built-in browser and go.

  • One custom look

    A restyled title screen, menus and HUD in a single dark, monochrome theme — consistent on every supported version.

  • Curated, not bloated

    A hand-picked set of QoL mods — zoom, freelook, fullbright, JEI and more. Toggle any of them from one menu. Nothing you didn't ask for.
